About Zuri

Built by someone who watched good businesses stay invisible.

Zuri started with a simple observation: some of the best businesses we know — tailors, roasters, leather workers, boutique studios — had incredible products and no way to be found online, no plan for what to post, and no way to stay consistent once the initial excitement wore off. Zuri exists to close that gap.

Our Mission

Every African SME deserves a real, well-run digital presence.

Most small businesses across Nigeria run on WhatsApp status updates and Instagram DMs — not for lack of ambition, but because the tools available were never built for them: expensive, generic, and disconnected from how they actually operate. Zuri is different. In one conversation, it generates a premium website, hands you a 90-day content plan tailored to your business, and keeps you accountable with a growth tracker — so showing up online stops being a full-time job you can't afford.

What Zuri Actually Does

Not just a website — an operating system for staying visible.

A premium website

Generated from a single conversation, live in minutes — no code, no designer, no waiting weeks.

A 90-day content plan

AI-prepared posts and assets delivered daily, so you always know exactly what to publish next.

A growth tracker + expert network

A consistency tracker that keeps you accountable, plus access to vetted Nigerian agencies when you need real human help.

How We Think

Three principles guide everything we build.

Local first

Built around the payment rails, languages, and habits Nigerian businesses already use — not retrofitted from a Western template.

No code, ever

If it requires a developer, we haven't finished building it yet.

Premium by default

Every business deserves a presence that looks like it belongs to a business ten times its size.
